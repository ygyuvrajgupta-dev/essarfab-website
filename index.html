FAQ

Common Questions About PUF Panels & Cold Storage

Everything you need to know about insulation panels, cold rooms & warehouse solutions

What is a PUF panel / PUFF sandwich panel?

A PUF panel (Polyurethane Foam panel), also called a PUFF panel or sandwich panel, has two metal skins with a rigid polyurethane/PIR foam core. It offers excellent thermal insulation (λ=0.021 W/mK), is fire-retardant, CFC-free, and widely used for cold storage, cold rooms, cleanrooms, warehouses and industrial buildings.

What PUF panel thickness is best for cold storage?

For cold storage and cold rooms, 80mm–150mm PUF sandwich panels are standard. For deep freeze below -18°C, 120mm–150mm is recommended. ESSARFAB supplies panels from 30mm to 150mm for all cold room applications.

Yes. ESSARFAB GREEN INDIA supplies and installs cold storage rooms, cold rooms, warehouse insulation panels and cleanrooms across all states in India. We offer complete turnkey solutions — design, supply, installation and after-sales.

What industries use sandwich insulation panels?

PUF/PIR sandwich insulation panels are used in: cold storage & cold rooms, food processing, pharma cleanrooms, agriculture warehouses, industrial sheds, data centres, modular offices and refrigerated logistics.

Are ESSARFAB PUF panels fire rated?

Yes. All ESSARFAB PUF panels are self-extinguishing per IS 12436 and available in FR grades up to 2-hour fire rating. The CFC/HCFC-free foam meets Class B fire safety standards.
