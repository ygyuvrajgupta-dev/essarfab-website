Overview

ESSARFAB GREEN INDIA manufactures premium quality Wall PUF Sandwich Panels using advanced polyurethane insulation technology. These panels provide excellent thermal insulation (λ = 0.021 W/mK), superior structural strength, and fast modular installation with cam-lock joints.

Available in thicknesses from 40 mm to 150 mm with PPGI, GI or stainless steel skins, our wall panels are the ideal choice for temperature-controlled environments across industries. All panels are CFC/HCFC-free and fire-rated as per IS 12436 standards.
