Overview

ESSARFAB GREEN INDIA manufactures premium quality insulated roof sandwich panels with rigid polyurethane (PUR/PIR) foam core and high-tensile steel skins. These panels provide excellent thermal insulation, weather resistance, structural strength, and fast installation with concealed fastening systems. Ideal for large-span warehouse roofing, industrial shed roofs, and cold storage roof applications across India.

Available in various trapezoidal profiles with 0.4mm to 0.6mm steel thickness, PVDF/SMP coating for UV resistance, and cam-lock joint systems for leak-proof installation.

Applications

  • Warehouse Roofing & Cladding
  • Industrial Shed Roofs
  • Cold Storage Roof Construction
  • Food Processing Plant Roofs
  • Logistics & Distribution Centres
  • Commercial Building Roofs
  • Agricultural Storage Roofs
  • Pharmaceutical Facility Roofs
