Overview

ESSARFAB GREEN INDIA manufactures high-performance insulated roof panels using advanced polyurethane (PUF/PIR) technology. These panels offer excellent thermal insulation, weather resistance and structural strength while significantly reducing energy consumption in buildings.

Our roof panels feature trapezoidal profiles for efficient rainwater drainage, cam-lock joining systems for rapid installation, and a wide range of coating options including PVDF, SMP, and RMP for long-lasting weather protection.
