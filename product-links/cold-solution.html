Overview

ESSARFAB GREEN INDIA provides complete turnkey cold storage and cold room solutions — from initial design consultation and engineering to panel manufacturing, on-site installation, and after-sales service. Our cold storage solutions are built using high-quality PUF/PIR insulated sandwich panels, precision-engineered doors, and advanced refrigeration systems.

With 30+ years of experience and 500+ successful projects across India, we are the preferred cold storage partner for agriculture, food processing, pharmaceutical, and logistics sectors.
