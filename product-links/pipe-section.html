Overview

ESSARFAB GREEN INDIA manufactures pre-formed rigid PUF (Polyurethane Foam) pipe insulation sections that provide superior thermal insulation for industrial piping systems. Our pipe sections are precision-moulded to fit standard pipe diameters and offer excellent thermal conductivity, moisture resistance, and mechanical strength.

Ideal for HVAC ducting, ammonia refrigeration lines, chilled water pipes, steam lines, and cryogenic applications. Available with aluminium foil, GI sheet, or stainless steel cladding.

Applications

  • HVAC & Air Conditioning Ductwork
  • Ammonia Refrigeration Pipelines
  • Chilled Water & Brine Lines
  • Cold Storage Pipe Insulation
  • Food Processing & Dairy Lines
  • Pharmaceutical Process Piping
  • Industrial Steam & Condensate Lines
  • Cryogenic & Low Temperature Lines
