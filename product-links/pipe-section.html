Overview

ESSARFAB GREEN INDIA manufactures high-quality pre-formed rigid PUF (Polyurethane Foam) insulated pipe sections designed to minimize heat loss, prevent condensation, and improve energy efficiency in industrial piping systems. Our pipe sections are manufactured with closed cell content ≥90%, ensuring superior thermal performance and moisture resistance.

Available in a wide range of diameters and thicknesses with aluminium, PPGI, or GI outer cladding, these pipe sections are ideal for both new installations and retrofit projects across all temperature-sensitive industries.
