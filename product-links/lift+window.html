Overview

Lift windows provide convenient material transfer between controlled environments while maintaining insulation and hygiene standards. They are widely used in food, pharmaceutical and industrial applications where quick access and controlled environments are required without compromising thermal integrity.

ESSARFAB's insulated lift windows are manufactured with PUF/PIR foam cores, double glazed toughened glass, and heavy-duty stainless steel hardware for smooth, reliable operation and long service life.

Types of Lift Windows

  • Vertical Lift Windows — Upward Opening
  • Horizontal Lift Windows — Sideways Sliding
  • Counter Balanced Lift Windows — Easy Operation
  • Manual Lift Windows — Simple & Reliable
  • Pass Box Lift Windows — For Clean Room Transfers
  • Customized Lift Windows — Built to Your Specifications
