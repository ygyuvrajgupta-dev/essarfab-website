Overview

ESSARFAB GREEN INDIA manufactures high-quality insulated lift windows designed for convenient material transfer between controlled environments — maintaining temperature, hygiene, and pressure differentials. Ideal for cold rooms, clean rooms, pharmaceutical facilities, and food processing plants where seamless material pass-through is essential.

Our lift windows feature double-glazed toughened glass, counter-balanced lift mechanisms, airtight sealing, and PUF insulated frames that prevent thermal bridging.

Applications

  • Cold Room Material Transfer
  • Clean Room Pass Box Windows
  • Pharmaceutical Production Lines
  • Food Processing & Packing Areas
  • Laboratory Sample Transfer
  • Warehouse Monitoring Windows
  • Hospital & Healthcare Facilities
